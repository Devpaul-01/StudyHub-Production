Questions? We Got You

⏰ When does StudyHub launch?

🎯 Launching December 25, 2025! Join our waitlist now to get exclusive early access!

💰 Is it actually free?

Yes! Core features (Q&A, Study Buddy, Basic Threads) are 100% free forever. Premium unlocks analytics, unlimited threads, and advanced matching.

🤔 How is this different from Discord?

Discord is for general chat. StudyHub is purpose-built for learning: organized Q&A with solutions, smart study partner matching, progress tracking, reputation system, and features designed specifically for academic collaboration.

🎓 Do I need to be at a specific university?

Nope! Any student with an email can join. We group people by subject and interest, not school.

📱 Will there be a mobile app?

Yes! Mobile apps for iOS and Android are coming shortly after web launch. Sign up to get notified.

🔒 How do you handle my data?

Your privacy is sacred. We never sell your data. Everything is encrypted. You control what's public vs private. See our privacy policy for details.
